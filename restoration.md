## Initial Inspection and Disassembly Notes

An initial photographic documentation of the entire device was performed in order to record its original condition before any intervention.

### Mechanical observations

- The platter rotates.
- The stylus is loose.
- The cartridge is disconnected.

### Initial electrical inspection

After removing the bottom cover, electrical measurements were performed.

Results:

- The mains suppression capacitor is short-circuited.
- Motor capacitors measure approximately **0.8 µF** out of **0.75 µF**.

### Mechanical disassembly

- The platter retaining clip was removed and the platter extracted.
- The motor circlips were removed.
- The speed selector linkage rod was disconnected.

Observation:

- The motor assembly requires servicing.

### Tonearm condition

- The tonearm wiring is broken.
- The system is **mono**.
- Excessive weight is present at the stylus end.

### Proposed modifications

- Replace the cartridge with a **magnetic phono cartridge**.
- Add a **fuse** to the mains power cable for safety.
- Replace capacitors with modern **X2 safety capacitors**:

| Component | Replacement |
|---|---|
| Mains suppression capacitor | 0.12 µF – 275V X2 |
| Motor capacitor | 0.68 µF – 275V X2 |
